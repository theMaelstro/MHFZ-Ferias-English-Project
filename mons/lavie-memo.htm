Raviente Hitzone Changes

During the quest, when attacked with one element, It will become resistant to that element after a certain amount.
When in rage the hitzone values of cutting, impact and shots change. Elements stay same as above.

Poison
Accumulated value is calculated for the entire land and when Poison support is sent, 20,000 fixed damage is applied.

Part Breaks
Head, Body and Tail all breakable multiple times.

Special Part Breaks
Damage is calculated through the entire land, when ready, destruction support can be called.
Only one part can be broken with destruction support at a time. There are no part break rewards, only extra contribution points.
