Hunter Jewel

Color
・There are eight colors of hunter beads: pink, brown, yellow, green, white, blue, light blue, and rainbow. Rainbow is a special color.
・The color will change during regular maintenance (every Wednesday).
　　※Apart from this, it may change to "rainbow color" at 12:00 noon
・If it is rainbow, it will change to another color at 12:00 the next day.

・In order to accumulate colors, clear route 101 and beyond.
・Only the number of colors of the people who participated in the quest will be added, but rainbow will change to all colors +1
　※Since the effects of rainbow colors do not overlap, all colors remain +1 no matter how many rainbow colors are present.

・Activation is required when the hunter jewel is strengthened beyond the second level.
・Once the Activation period has passed, you will no longer be able to use the Hunter Jewel skill.(outdated?)

Skill
・Can activate up to 3 skills with a total cost of up to 10
